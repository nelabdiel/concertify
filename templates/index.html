ConcertiPy Web App

ConcertiPy chooses an artist you follow on Spotify and shows your their next concert.

Using the Spotify API, ConcertiPy pulls an artists you follow at random and displays their next concert. The goal for ConcertiPy is to eventually link to the Stubhub API also to pull a list of concerts of the artists you follow on Spotify, based on proximity of both distance and date.
